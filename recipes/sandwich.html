Sandwich

How to make a deicious sandwich

Step one: Choose bread

For the first step, be sure to choose a bread that will suit the sandwich you are trying to make. Some selections are pretzle bread, wheat bread, white bread, and my personal favorite, sour dough.

Step two: Choose meat

The meat you choose can make or break your sandwich, so choose wisely. If you want a cold sandwich, I suggest turkey, if you want a warm sandwich, I'd say ham, both are great choices, but feel free to choose whatever you'd like as long as it's logical.

Step three: Combine

Whether you're eating it cold or heating it up, you must combine your meat and bread, it's simple, just make sure to not leave too mush hanging off the sides.
